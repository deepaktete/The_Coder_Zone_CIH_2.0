What it is

The Goal & Habits module helps users define clear objectives and track the small daily actions required to achieve them. This system focuses on consistency by turning big goals into manageable habits that build progress over time.

Its Importance

Setting goals is easy, but maintaining habits is where true growth happens. Tracking habits not only improves discipline and focus, but also creates a measurable path toward self-improvement. Over time, this reduces mental fatigue, improves decision-making, and increases motivation.
